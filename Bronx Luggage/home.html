ABOUT US

Bronx Luggage is a renowned luggage store that has been providing high-quality travel gear to customers for many years. We understand that receiving high-quality luggage is an amazing feeling, and that's why we offer a wide selection of luggage sets from affordable to high-end brands.
Our store is located in the Festival Marketplace, making it easy for our customers to find us and access our products. We believe that great luggage is not just a travel accessory but also an investment that can be passed down from family members who are experienced travelers. That's why we take pride in offering a selection of high-quality sets of luggage that will last a lifetime.
Thank you for choosing Bronx Luggage for your travel needs. We look forward to serving you!
